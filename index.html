Welcome! This is Xiaobin Chang's Homepage. He is an Associate Professor at School of Artificial Intelligence, Sun Yat-sen University. He was a postdoc researcher of Simon Fraser University and work with Professor Greg Mori. He got Ph.D. degree from Queen Mary University of London, under the guidance of Professor Tao Xiang and Timothy Hospedales. His Master advisor was Professor Wei-Shi Zheng at Sun Yat-sen University. His research interests including: Computer Vision (e.g., Action Analysis and Representation Learning) & Machine Learning (e.g., Temporal Information Porcessing and Multi-View Problems)

招生

我的研究领域主要是计算机视觉(Computer Vision)和机器学习(Machine Learning)，过往的研究侧重于利用机器学习的方法来分析图像中的人物及其相关信息，现在还对持续学习和AIGC(如扩散模型)相关研究方向感兴趣。欢迎联系和咨询硕士研究生相关事宜，也欢迎有意愿提前进入本实验室的本科生联系。【请首选学校邮箱发信联系，至少应附上以下材料：成绩单、外语成绩（包括但不限于英语四六级）、重要奖项证书等，供参考。】 联系方式：changxb3_at_mail.sysu.edu.cn；
